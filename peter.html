Peter Poston

Peter is a mix of beagle and corgi. He was rescued from the West LA Shelter on Pico Blvd. He was found on the streets, but he once lived in a home. He reacts to the sounds of door-bells on tv, which is evidence he once lived somewhere with a door-bell. He is 8 years-old and he has been a good friend of mine for the past year and 3 months since we picked him up from the shelter at half price. He had been at the shelter for over a month by the time he came home with us.
